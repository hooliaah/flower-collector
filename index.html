Flower Collector!

A random number will be shown at the start of the game.

By clicking on one of the four flowers, a certain amount of points will be added to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each flower is hidden until you click on it. At the start of each game, the value of each flower will change.
